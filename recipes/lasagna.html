Lasagna

Description

What's better than some sweet sweet loving from Garfield's very own favorite dish? Lasagna has been the cornerstone of civilization and been the center of love and war. After all, are you telling me Romans were only fighting for the throne (come on)? What are you waiting for, let's get started preparing the meal of the Gods!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ground Beef (or veggie alternative)
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Paste
  • Most importantly...Love

Steps

  1. Take Pasta as a base and with layers spread tomato paste, followed by beef and cheese
  2. Repeat for a couple of layers
  3. Set in oven and set to bake for 15 minutes at high heat or 30min on medium-high
  4. Cry from how wonderful it tastes (I know, no need to thank me)
