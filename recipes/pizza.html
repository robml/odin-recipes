Pizza

Description

Mamma mia! It's a wonderful thing to eat-a-the-pizza! Hold on to your seats ladies and gentlement because today we will be making a dish that has been the controversy of Queens and mobsters all the way to drunk sports fans grabbing a bite. That's right, it's pizza time.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Mozarella
  • Tomato Paste
  • Those Soft Massaging Hands of Yours

Steps

  1. Take your beautifully soft hands and artisanally massage the dough in a circle
  2. Spread the tomato sauce with care just as the calm river in Pocahontas did
  3. Sprinkle the mozarella Salt-Bae style like your life depended on it
  4. Bake in high heat oven for 5-15min and voila, it's a masterpiece!
